5 Hacks to Maximize Your Swagbucks.com Earnings



Swagbucks.com is a great way to earn rewards for doing things you already do online, like taking surveys, watching videos, and shopping through their portal. While the earning potential is relatively low, it doesn't take much time to earn a decent number of points. Swagbucks, "SB," can be redeemed for gift cards or PayPal cash. Here are five hacks to help you earn more Swagbucks.



1. Use the SwagButton extension: The SwagButton is a browser extension that will notify you whenever there's an opportunity to earn rewards on a website you're visiting. It's available for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge browsers. Install the extension and start earning rewards with minimal effort.



2. Take advantage of sign-up bonuses: You'll automatically be eligible for sign-up bonuses when you create a new account on Swagbucks.com. These bonuses usually consist of a few hundred points. Swagbucks, "SB," can be redeemed for gift cards or PayPal cash. Take advantage of these bonuses by creating new accounts whenever offered.



3. Complete surveys during peak times: Survey opportunities tend to be more plentiful during certain times and weeks. Evenings and weekends are when you'll have the most luck finding surveys to complete. Set aside sometime each week to conduct surveys, and you'll start racking up the points in no time.



4. Shop through the Swagbucks portal: Whenever you need to make an online purchase, be sure to do it through the Swagbucks portal. You'll earn rewards for every dollar you spend, which can add up quickly if you make a large purchase. Plus, there are often bonus opportunities for shopping through the portal, so keep an eye out for those.



5. Refer your friends and family members: One of the easiest ways to earn more points on Swagbucks is by referring your friends and family to the site. There's no limit to how many people you can refer, so start spreading the word today! For every person who signs up using your referral link, you'll earn a percentage of the points they accumulate while using the site.



If you're looking for an easy way to earn extra rewards, check out Swagbucks.com. Following these simple hacks, you can quickly maximize your earnings and get more bang for your buck!
